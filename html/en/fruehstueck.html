Attention


Changes in breakfast procedure

Due to Covid-19 (Coronavirus) there will be changes in our breakfast procedure.

Instead of the buffet we will serve to breakfast directly to your table or room.

The amount of seatable tables is limited because of the given minimum distance. This leads to less available tables in our breakfast room. This leads to time-slots for each room and table. When you check-in you will be asked to fill in a formula where you can select your customized breakfast and individual timeslot.

Due to capacity reasons we are not able to offer this service to our apartment guests unless you booked this options before the pandemic.

Breakfast room

Start your day the best way.

The breakfast is the most important meal of the day and you should start your day the best way possible. From 8:00 to 10:00 we offer a comprehensive buffet in our main hotel Rungholt. We are sure you find something that suits your taste on our buffet, but if not we are happy to prepare something for you after some arrangement.

From our breakfast room and terrace you can enjoy the amazing view directly onto the north-sea.

Guest of our apartments can also eat at our hotel for an extra fee of 15,00 EUR for an adult and 8,00 EUR for children up to 12 years.
