Apartments

Our apartments are great if you like to be more independent and feel like home.

For an extra fee you can also book the hotel-service with additional cleaning and the great breakfast buffet. Ask our staff for more information. You can also get an extra bed for an additional fee. The general price is for one night. Note that there is a 20 percent discount when booking for off-season.

Every apartment is fitted with a satellite TV.

Plase understand that because of respect for allergy sufferers we do not allow pets in our hotel rooms and apartments.

An Extra bet is can be requested and will cost 40 Euro for every day and Person. For children from 8 to 14 years it costs 25 Euro.
