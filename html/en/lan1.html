Internet & Fernsehen

Internet

The internet in our hotel is provided by the service of lan1. You will have a wireless connection in every room and inside the breakfast room. Note that for using that service you need to agree to lan1 terms of service and give an e-mail address and name.

TV

Every room is equipped with a TV that is connected to satellite TV. This allows to receive hundreds of channels. You are also able to use the tv as a radio.
