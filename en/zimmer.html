Rooms at Hotel Rungholt

All our hotel rooms are high-quality, modern and in a soothing maritime coloring. Small differences in furnishing, size and view allow you to pick a room that suits your needs and preferences the most. Explorer our rooms on this website and pick the room you like.

All prices are for the room and night. The price contains the breakfast from our buffet. K Kids under the age of seven are not charged extra when staying in the room of the parents. In some rooms it is possible to get an extra bed, feel free to ask us about this service.

There is a 20% discount when booking for off-season.
Every room comes with a satellite TV
All rooms (Except the Einzelzimmer.Einfach) have a bathroom with a shower.

Please understand that in consideration of allergy sufferers we do not allow pets in our hotel.
